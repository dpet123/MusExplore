Frequently Asked Questions


How can I create my MusExplore account?

Create your account by clicking on the Signup button near the top of our page, and follow the instructions.

How can I delete my MusExplore account?

Delete your account by visiting the login page and follow the instructions after clicking the "delete account" link.

How can I reset my MusExplore password?

Go to the login page and click "forgot password" and follow the on-screen instructions.
